Pretzels

Pastry

Prep Time: 45 mins Cook Time: 10-15 mins Total Time: 2 hours Servings: 16 Source: BakedIn Box

Ingredients

350ml milk

70g unsalted butter (plus extra melted to coat when cooked)

570g strong white bread flour

30g caster sugar

7g yeast

2 tbsp bicarbonate of soda

Topping

2 tsp cinnamon

65g caster sugar

Directions

1) Melt 70g butter in a small pan and add the milk. On a low, heat warm the mixture until it is just past lukewarm (~40 C, or a comfortable temperature for your finger). If it's too hot leave to cool.

2) Mix the flour, sugar, salt and yeast in a large bowl.

3) Make a well in the flour mix and pour in the warmed milk and butter. Mix together with a wooden spoon until a soft dough is formed/

4) Sprinkle 1 tbsp of flour onto a work surface and tip the dough onto it. Knead the dough for 8-10 minutes until smooth and elastic (dough is quite wet but stops sticking as you knead)

5) Shape the dough into a bowl and place in a greased bowl. Cover and leave in a warm place for 60-90 mins

6) Empty the dough onto a clean surface and knead to knock out the air and dived into 16 equal balls

7) Roll the balls into 50 cm ropes and shape (see picture for technique)

8) Line baking sheet with baking paper and preheat oven to 180C/ gas mark 6

9) Add boiling water (~10cm deep) to a pan with bicarb and simmer. Lower pretzels into water and cook for 10 seconds on each side. Remove, drain and place on baking sheet.

10) Bake for 10-15 mins until golden brown

11) Brush cooked pretzels with melted butter and toss in cinnamon sugar mix

Notes

For chocolate dipping sauce melt 70g chocolate in 35ml milk.
Coat in garlic butter or course ground salt for savoury version.
