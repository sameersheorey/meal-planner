Spiced Chicken Wraps with Mint Chutney

Chicken, Indian

Prep Time: 25 min Total Time: 25 min Servings: 2 Source: Gousto.co.uk

Ingredients

1 lemon

1 red onion

1 tomato

6 white tortilla wraps

1 tsp nigella seeds

1 tsp ground turmeric

1/2 tsp cayenne pepper

1 tbsp curry powder

5g coriander

5g mint

80g baby leaf spinach

2tbsp mayonnaise

250g chicken breast fillets

Description

For an Indian-inspired twist on your favourite chicken wrap, try these delicious soft tortillas stuffed with fiery curried chicken and spinach. Finish with a fresh mint chutney and tomato salsa, that's a wrap!

Directions

1) Preheat the oven to 180°C (fan)/ Gas 6

2) Cut the chicken breasts into bite size pieces and add to a bowl with the curry powder, ground turmeric, cayenne pepper and a generous pinch of salt. Give everything a good mix up so that the chicken is fully coated in the spices

3) Peel and finely slice the red onion

4) Heat a large, wide-based pan with a drizzle of vegetable oil over a medium-low heat. Once hot, add 3/4 of the sliced onion with a pinch of salt and cook for 10-12 min or until softened

5) While the onion is cooking, chop the coriander roughly, including the stalks. Pick the mint leaves from their stalks and chop roughly, discarding the stalks. Add the chopped coriander and mint to a food processor with a small squeeze of lemon and a pinch of salt. Grind to a fine paste, then add to the mayonnaise and a drizzle of olive oil – this is your mint chutney

6) Dice the tomato and add the diced tomato to a bowl with the nigella seeds, remaining red onion and a drizzle of olive oil. Season with a pinch of salt and give everything a good mix up – this is your tomato salad

7) Once the onion has softened, increase the heat to medium-high. Add the chicken to the pan and cook for 8 min or until cooked through. Squeeze in the juice of the remaining lemon and mix to combine – this is your spiced chicken

8) While the chicken is cooking, add the tortillas to a baking tray and put the tray in the oven for 3-4 min or until warmed through

9) Heat a separate large, wide-based pan over a medium-high heat. Once hot, add the baby leaf spinach and stir for 1 min or until it's beginning to wilt very slightly, then remove from the heat

10) Serve the warmed tortillas topped with the wilted spinach, spiced chicken and tomato salad. Drizzle with the mint chutney
