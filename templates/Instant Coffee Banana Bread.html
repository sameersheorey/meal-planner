Instant Coffee Banana Bread

Baking, Cakes

Prep Time: less than 30 mins Cook Time: 30 mins to 1 hour Servings: Serves 6-8 Source: Bbc.co.uk

Ingredients

3 very ripe bananas

110g soft brown sugar

2 free-range eggs

1 tbsp instant coffee

Pinch nutmeg/cinnamon/1 tbsp finely chopped stem ginger

85g unsalted butter, melted

225g plain flour and 1 tbsp baking powder

100g chocolate pieces (optional)

Directions

1) Preheat the oven to 170C and grease a 1 litre loaf tin

2) Mash the bananas in a bowl with the sugar, eggs, coffee, nutmeg and salt until everything is thoroughly combined.

3) Melt the butter and stir in with the flour and chocolate, if using.

4) Pour into the tin and bake for 50 minutes.

5) Remove from the oven and leave to cool slightly in the tin. Turn out onto a wire rack to cool completely and then serve in slices.
