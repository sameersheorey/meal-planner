Herby Chicken with Red Onion Mash and Garlicky Green Beans

Chicken, Dinner

Total Time: 50 min Servings: 2 Source: Hellofresh.co.uk

Ingredients

2 potatos

1 red onion

1 pack green beans

1 bunch flat leaf parsley

10g panko breadcrumbs

2 chicken breasts

1 tsp provencale herbs

1 tbsp mayonnaise

1 tbsp balsamic vinegar

1 garlic clove

75 ml water

Directions

1) Preheat your oven to 200°C

2) Put a large saucepan of water on to boil with 0.5 tsp of salt.

3) Chop the potato into 2cm chunks (no need to peel!), pop the potato into the boiling water and simmer for 15-20 mins

4) Halve, peel and thinly slice the red onion.

5) Trim the green beans.

7) Roughly chop the parsley (stalks and all). Put half the parsley in a small bowl and mix in the panko breadcrumbs, Provençale herbs, 1 tbsp olive oil and season with salt.

8) Pop the chicken breasts on a baking tray and season with salt and black pepper. Using the back of a spoon, spread the mayo over the top half of each breast and then sprinkle over the crumb mixture.

9) Roast the chicken on the top shelf of your oven until cooked through and golden on top, 25-30 mins.

10) Meanwhile, heat a splash of oil in a frying pan over medium heat. Add the onion. Cook until soft, 7-10 mins, stirring occasionally, then remove from the pan and keep to one side. Don't wash the pan - we'll use it later to cook the green beans.

11) Once the potatoes are cooked, drain in a colander and return to the pan off the heat. Add a knob of butter and a splash of milk (if you have any) and mash until smooth. Season with salt and pepper and stir in the cooked onion. Cover with a lid to keep warm.

12) Heat a splash of oil in your (now empty) frying pan on high heat. Stir-fry the green beans for 2 mins.

13) Add the crushed garlic and season with salt then add the water and pop a lid on the pan and steam until just tender, 3-5 mins.

14) Drain any leftover water, then stir through the balsamic vinegar and 1 tbsp olive oil

15) Reheat the onion mash if necessary and spoon a generous amount onto your plate. Top it with the chicken and place some garlicky green beans alongside. Drizzle the balsamic dressing from the beans around the plate and finish with a sprinkling of the remaining parsley. Enjoy!
