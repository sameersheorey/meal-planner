Tom yum soup

Dinner, Seafood, Thai

Ingredients

600ml vegetable stock

1 stick of lemongrass (can use dried if can't get fresh)

6 lime leaves

6 ripe cherry tomatoes

5cm piece of galangal (can sub with ginger)

1 fresh green bird’s-eye chilli

1 teaspoon palm sugar

1 tablespoon fish sauce

1 clove of garlic

1 shallot

6 raw peeled kings prawns

2 large oyster mushrooms (can sub with any mushroom)

1 lime

Directions

1) Bring the stock to a boil.

2) Bash the lemongrass, rip the lime leaves, halve the tomatoes, and add to the stock with the galangal, chilli, palm sugar and fish sauce.

3) Mince the garlic and dice the shallot, then add to the stock. Leave the soup to simmer gently for at least 20 minutes.

4) Add the prawns and tear in the oyster mushrooms. Squeeze in the juice of the lime. Bring to a high heat until the prawns are cooked.

5) Taste, and season if needed. Adjust the salt, sour, sweet and heat to your preference.

6) Remove the lemongrass, lime leaves, galangal and chilli
