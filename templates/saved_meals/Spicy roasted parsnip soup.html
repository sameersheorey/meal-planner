Spicy roasted parsnip soup

Lunch

Prep Time: 10 mins Cook Time: 35 mins Servings: 4 Source: Bbcgoodfood.com

Ingredients

2 tbsp olive oil

1 tsp coriander seeds

1 tsp cumin seeds, plus extra to garnish

½ tsp ground turmeric

½ tsp mustard seeds

1 large onion

2 garlic cloves

675g parsnips

2 plum tomatoes

1.2l vegetable stock

1 tbsp lemon juice

Description

Aromatic flavours transform the ordinary parsnip into a delicious warming soup

Directions

1) Heat oven to 200C (fan)

2) In a bowl, mix together olive oil, coriander seeds, cumin seeds, turmeric and mustard seeds.

3) Dice the parsnips, cut the onion into 8 chunks, and quarter the tomatoes.

4) Spread on a baking sheet with the garlic, mix well, then roast for 30 mins until tender.

5) Mix with 600ml vegetable stock and process with stick blender until smooth.

6) Pour into a pan with the remaining 600ml vegetable stock, season, then heat until barely simmering.

7) Remove from the heat and stir in lemon juice. Garnish with cumin seeds.
