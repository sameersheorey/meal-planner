White chocolate and lemon fudge icing

Baking, Extras

Prep Time: 5 mins Cook Time: 10 mins Servings: Yields: 36 squares Source: Lifeloveliz.com

Ingredients

250g white chocolate

1/2 can (397g) condensed milk

60g butter

1 lemon (zest and juice)

Directions

1) Add the chocolate chips, condensed milk, lemon juice, lemon zest and butter into a medium pot over low to medium heat

2) Stir mixture until melted

3) Leave to cool slightly then pour onto cake and spread.

4) Leave out to set overnight
