Tomato, spinach and mozzarella pasta bake

Dinner, Italian, Vegetarian

Servings: 4 Source: thekitchn

Ingredients

For the pasta and sauce:

1 tbsp olive oil

1 brown onion, finely chopped

2 cloves garlic, finely chopped (or 1 cube frozen garlic)

1/2 tsp chilli flakes

500g passata

4 cubes of frozen spinach

1/2 tsp salt

250g dried pasta

125g whole-milk ricotta cheese

25g grated Parmesan cheese

110g shredded mozzarella cheese

Directions

1) Preheat the oven to 190°C.

2) Boil the pasta in a large pot of heavily salted water over medium-high heat and cook until just tender.

3) Heat the oil in a large pot, when hot add the onion, garlic, and chilli flakes, season with salt and pepper, and cook, stirring occasionally, until softened, about 12 minutes.

4) Add the passata, spinach and 1/2 tsp salt, stir to combine, and bring to a simmer. Remove from the heat.

5) Stir the ricotta and half the Parmesan together in a small bowl.

6) Drain pasta and return to pot with half the sauce and stir until well coated

7) Add the ricotta mixture to the pasta and fold it in, leaving big pockets of ricotta here and there.

8) Transfer half of the pasta mixture into a baking dish and spread into an even layer. Evenly sprinkle with half of the mozzarella. Top with the remaining pasta in an even layer.

9) Pour the remaining sauce over the pasta and spread into an even layer. Sprinkle the surface evenly with the remaining mozzarella and parmesan.

10) Bake uncovered until the edges are bubbling and the cheese is completely melted and browned in spots, about 30 minutes.

11) Remove the baking dish to a wire rack and let cool 15 minutes before serving.
