Courgette blondies

★★★★★

Baking, Cakes

Prep Time: 25 minutes Cook Time: 30 minutes Source: Creationsbykara.com

Ingredients

150g butter

440g brown sugar

1 egg

2 tsp vanilla

250g plain flour

1/2 tsp baking powder

1/4 tsp bicarb soda

1/2 tsp salt

1 1/2 tsp cinnamon

175g courgette

160g white chocolate chips

Directions

1) Cook butter in a saucepan over medium heat till it starts to turn golden brown, stirring occasionally.

2) Add the brown sugar. Remove from heat and let cool for 5-10 minutes.

3) Grate the courgette

4) Pour butter/sugar mixture into a mixing bowl. Whisk in the egg and vanilla.

5) Add the dry ingredients and mix together.

6) Stir in the courgette and chocolate chips.

7) Spread batter in a well greased 9x13" pan.

8) Bake in a preheated 180°C oven for about 30 minutes or till the middle is set.
