Nutty Rice Salad

Chinese, Lunch

Total Time: 25 min Servings: 2 Source: Gousto.co.uk

Ingredients

1 little gem lettuce

3 tbsp soy sauce

2 spring onions

1/2 cucumber

100g basmati rice

1 carrot

1 tbsp toasted sesame oil

1 tbsp rice vinegar

1 tbsp smooth peanut butter

20g toasted sesame seeds

Directions

1) Add the basmati rice and 250ml [500ml] water to a pot with a lid and bring to the boil over a high heat. Once boiling, reduce the heat to very low and cook, covered, for 10-15 min or until all the water has been absorbed and the rice is cooked, then remove from the heat and keep covered until serving

2) Shred the little gem finely

3) Cut the cucumber into matchsticks

4) Top, tail, peel, then grate the carrot

5) Trim, then slice the spring onions finely

6) Add the little gem, carrot, cucumber and spring onion to a large mixing bowl

7) Combine the peanut butter, soy sauce, toasted sesame oil, rice vinegar and chilli flakes in a small bowl

8) Add 1 tbsp [2 tbsp] boiled water with 1 tsp [2 tsp] sugar and mix to combine – this is your peanut dressing

9) Fluff the cooked rice with a fork and add it to the crunchy salad. Dress the crunchy salad with the peanut dressing and a drizzle of olive oil and season with a generous pinch of salt
