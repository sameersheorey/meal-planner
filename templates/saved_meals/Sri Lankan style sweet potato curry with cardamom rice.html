Sri Lankan style sweet potato curry with cardamom rice

Dinner, Vegan

Servings: 4

Ingredients

2 brown onions

6 cardamom pods

2 tbsp tandoori masala

30g root ginger

4 garlic cloves

2 green chilli

1 tsp ground cinnamon

1 tsp ground turmeric

50g creamed coconut

2 sweet potatoes

2 tbsp tomato puree

2 vegetable stock cubes

425ml water (for stock)

260g basmati rice

600ml water (for rice)


Optional (non vegan)

200g crab meat

Directions

1) Peel onions and finely chop (use electric chopper)

2) Chop sweet potato into bite-sized pieces

3) Heat a large wide-based pan with lid with drizzle of vegetable oil over a medium heat. Once hot add onions with a pinch of salt and cook for 5-6 mins until softened

4) Boil kettle

5) Peel ginger and garlic and finely chop (use electric chopper)

6) Crush cardamom pods with side of knife

7) Slice chilli finely

8) Dissolver creamed coconut, stock cubes, tomato puree in boiled water

9) Add garlic, ginger, tandoori masala, turmeric, cinnamon to pan with onions. Cook for 1 min until fragrant

10) Add sweet potato, coconut stock and a pinch of sugar.

11) Bring to boil, then reduce heat and cook covered for 15-18 mins until sweet potato is soft

12) Add rice, cardamom pods, cold water and pinch of salt to a pot with a lid and bring to a boil over a high heat. Reduce heat to verly low and cook covered for 10-12 mins. Remove from heat and keep covered until serving

13) OPTIONAL: Stir crab into curry if using

14) Serve the curry over the rice and garnish with chilli
