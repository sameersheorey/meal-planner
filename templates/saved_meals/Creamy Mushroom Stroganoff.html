Creamy Mushroom Stroganoff

Dinner, Russian, Vegetarian

Total Time: 25 min Servings: 2 Source: Gousto.co.uk

Ingredients

1 red onion

1 garlic clove

5g thyme

2 tsp smoked paprika

1 vegetable stock cube

190g linguine

250g chestnut mushrooms

100g crème fraîche

Description

In this silky vegetarian stroganoff, you'll smother smoky caramelised mushrooms in a thyme-infused crème fraîche sauce. Toss with linguine, season with plenty of pepper and serve. Delicious!

Directions

1) Peel and finely slice the red onion

2) Heat a large, wide-based pan with a drizzle of olive oil and 1-2 tbsp butter over a medium heat. Once melted, add the sliced onion with a pinch of salt and cook for 10 min or until soft

3) While the onion is cooking, boil a kettle

4) Peel and finely chop the garlic

5) Cut the chestnut mushrooms into quarters

6) Strip the thyme leaves from their stems and chop them finely, discard the stems

7) Add the chopped garlic, mushrooms, half of the smoked paprika and half of the chopped thyme to the pan. Season with salt and a generous amount of pepper. Spread the mushrooms out as much as possible and allow to cook without stirring for 5-10 min or until caramelised

8) Meanwhile, add the linguine to a pot of boiled water with a large pinch of salt and bring to the boil over a high heat. Cook the linguine for 8-10 min or until cooked with a slight bite. Once done, drain the linguine, reserving a cup of the starchy pasta water

9) While the pasta is cooking, dissolve the vegetable stock cube in 200ml [400ml] boiled water and mix well

10) Combine the remaining chopped thyme, the crème fraîche and a generous amount of pepper in a separate bowl and mix well – this is your seasoned crème fraîche

11) Add half of the vegetable stock to the pan, increase the heat to medium-high and cook until the stock has completely absorbed

12) Add the remaining smoked paprika and stir well

13) Add the drained linguine to the pan. Add the remaining stock, and cook until reduced and thickened in consistency, then add the seasoned crème fraîche and stir to combine – this is your creamy mushroom stroganoff

Tip: Add a splash of the reserved starchy water if the sauce is looking a little dry

14) Serve the creamy mushroom stroganoff and season with pepper
