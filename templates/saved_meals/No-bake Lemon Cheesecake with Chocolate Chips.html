No-bake Lemon Cheesecake with Chocolate Chips

Desserts

Prep Time: 10 Total Time: 190 Source: Allrecipes.co.uk

Ingredients

200g digestive biscuits

50g unsalted butter

300g cream cheese (original or light)

300g crème fraiche

50-100g caster sugar

1 whole lemon

100g dark chocolate chips

Directions

1) Crunch up the biscuits in a food processor, You want it all to be very fine and no large lumps.

2) Melt the butter, add to the biscuits and mix until it is all even. Put the biscuit mix in a round spring-loaded tin and pat down nice and tight, right to the edge.

3) Place in a freezer to chill quickly, as it needs to be cold before putting on the cheesecake.

4) Put the cream cheese in a bowl and mix it until it is soft.

5) Add the crème fraiche and mix again until it is softer still.

6) Add the sugar, The amount is up to you. if you want it sweet add more, if you like the sourness of the lemon, add less.

7) Zest the lemon, and mix into cream cheese

8) Juice the lemon and add this to the mix. Mix in as quick as you can until you have a lovely smelling smooth mix.

9) Add the chocolate chips

10) Take the base out of the freezer and smooth the cheesecake onto the base. Put it in the fridge, it will be ready to eat and set in about 3 hours.
