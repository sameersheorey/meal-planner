Rigatoni Caprese with Pine Nuts, Mozzarella and Balsamic Tomato Salsa

Dinner, Italian, Vegetarian

Total Time: 30 min Servings: 2 Source: Hellofresh.co.uk

Ingredients

1 shallot

1 clove garlic

250g baby plum tomatoes

1 bunch flat leaf parsley

200g rigatoni

25g pine nuts

1 tbsp tomato puree

1 tin chopped tomatoes

1 vegetable stock cube

1 bunch chives

1 ball mozzarella

1 tbsp balsamic vinegar

1tbsp honey

1 tbsp olive oil for the dressing

Directions

1) Bring a large saucepan of water to the boil with 0.5 tsp salt for the pasta.

2) Halve, peel and thinly slice the shallot.

3) Peel and grate the garlic

4) Halve the baby plum tomatoes

5) Roughly chop the parsley (stalks and all).

6) Place the tomatoes and parsley in a bowl and season with a pinch of salt.

7) Add the pasta to your boiling water and boil for 12 mins. Once cooked, drain the pasta in a colander, pop back in the pan, drizzle with oil and stir through to stop it sticking together.

8) Meanwhile, heat a large frying pan on medium heat and add the pine nuts (no oil!). Toast them until golden, 2-3 mins, tossing regularly. When golden, transfer them from your pan to a bowl. Tip: Watch the nuts like a hawk to make sure they don't burn!

9) Once the pine nuts are removed from your pan, put it back on medium heat and add a drizzle of oil.

10) Add the shallot to your pan, stir and cook until softened, 4-5 mins.

11) Add the garlic and tomato puree, stir and cook for 1 minute more.

12) Pour in the finely chopped tomatoes and stock cube and stir to dissolve. Add a pinch of sugar (optional). Bring to a simmer and cook until reduced by half, 10 mins.

13) While your sauce cooks, finely chop the chives.

14) Drain the mozzarella and pull it apart into small pieces.

15) Stir the balsamic vinegar, honey and olive oil into the bowl of tomatoes. Season to taste with salt and pepper.

16) When your sauce is cooked, season to taste with salt and pepper, then add the drained pasta and toss together.

17) Stir through half the chives and half the mozzarella then share into bowls.

18) Spoon your tomato salsa on top along with the remaining mozzarella, remaining chives and a sprinkle of pine nuts.

Notes

1 of 5 a day
