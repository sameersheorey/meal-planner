Christmas pudding

Christmas

Servings: 2x 2 pint puddings Source: Mum

Ingredients

8 oz margarine, melted

7 oz flour

8 oz soft brown sugar

12 oz currants

8 oz raisins

6 oz sultanas

2 oz mixed peel

1 oz flaked almonds

1 oz glace cherries, chopped

6 oz fresh breadcrumbs

1 grated lemon rind

1 tsp nutmeg

1 tbsp black treacle

2 large eggs

3 tbsp milk

2 tbsp rum

Directions

1) Mix all the ingredients together and beat well (make a wish!)

2) Grease a 2x 2-pint pudding basins (or 1x 3 pint)

Slow cooker
3) Cover with clingfilm and then lid

4) Cook in slow cooker on HIGH for 6 hours or on LOW overnight

Pressure cooker
3) Stand the basin on the trivet and pour in 3 pints of boiling water. Fit lid without the weight and steam over a low heat for 20 minutes

4) Fit the weight and bring to pressure and cook for 2 hours at 15 lb (high) pressure. Reduce pressure slowly

5) Cool to room temperature, wrap in greaseproof paper, clingfilm and foil and store in cool dry place
