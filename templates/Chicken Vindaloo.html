Chicken Vindaloo

Chicken, Dinner, Indian

Prep Time: 13hrs Cook Time: 2hrs Total Time: 14hrs Servings: 6 Source: Rick Stein's India

Ingredients

750g chicken thighs/drumsticks

For the marinade

5 cm cinnamon stick

1 tsp black peppercorns

1 tsp cloves

1 tsp cumin seeds

12 green cardamoms

1 small onion

8 cloves garlic

25g ginger

2 tbsp tamarind liquid

1.5 tsp salt

0.5 tsp sugar

2 tbsp Kashmiri chilli powder

0.5 tsp turmeric

5 tbsp white wine vinegar

For the curry

2 tbsp ghee (or vegetable oil)

1 small onion - chopped

3 medium tomatoes - sliced

3 fresh green chillies - halved lengthways, seeds removed

100ml water

Directions

1) To make the marinade grind the cinnamon, black peppercorns, cloves, cumin seeds and cardamom seeds.

2) Put onion, garlic, tamarind, salt, sugar, chilli powder, turmeric, vinegar and the ground spices into a food processor and blend to a paste

3) Mix the chicken and the paste together in a large bowl, coating the chicken well. Cover the bowl and transfer to the fridge for 12 hours.

4) To cook the vindaloo, heat the ghee in a large, sturdy pan over a medium heat.

5) Add the onion, fry for 10 minutes until golden

6) Increase the heat to medium-high, add the chicken and all it's marinade paste and fry for 5 minutes, stirring occasionally

7) Stir in the tomatoes, green chillies and water, cover with a lid and cook for around 2 hours until the meat is tender. Check every so often that it's not dry or sticking, in which case add a splash of water
