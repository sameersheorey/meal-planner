Pau Bhaji

Dinner, Indian, Lunch

Servings: 4 Source: Adapted from Rick Stein's India

Ingredients

350g potatoes + veg (carrots and cauliflower work well)

65g butter

1 onion

2 tsp cumin seeds

400g tomatoes

300g can marrowfat peas

3 tsp pau bhaji masala

1 tsp chilli powder

1 tsp ground coriander

1.5 tsp salt

Fresh coriander

Fresh bread rolls

1 lime

1 red onion

Directions

1) Peel potatoes and veg and chop into large chunks, cook in pressure cooker for 10-15 minutes until soft.

2) Drain and mash.

2) Heat butter in a large wide based pan over a medium heat. Add the onions and cumin seeds and fry for 10 minutes until softened and golden.

3) Stir in the mashed veg and fry for 1-2 minutes.

4) Add the tomatoes, mix well and cook for 5 minutes, stirring often.

5) Add the marrowfat peas, all the spices and salt and cook for a further 5 minutes, stirring and mashing the mixture together.

6) Finish by stirring through the remaining 15g butter, the juice of the limes and a handful of chopped coriander.

7) Serve with generously buttered rolls, more coriander and diced red onion.
