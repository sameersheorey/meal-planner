Joe’s Popeye’s Chicken With Crispy Potatoes

Chicken

Total Time: 30 Servings: 2 Source: Gousto

Ingredients

1 garlic clove

250g cherry tomatoes

80g baby leaf spinach

1/2 chicken stock cube

50g soft cheese

4 white potatoes

2 x 125g chicken breast fillets

Description

"This one's from my Lean in 15: The Shape Plan, and it's comfort food with a twist! Serve up lean chicken, creamy spinach sauce, cherry tomatoes and crispy roasties. Banging!"

Directions

1) Preheat the oven to 200°C (fan)/ Gas 7

2) Chop the potatoes into roughly bite-sized pieces

3) Add the chopped potatoes and cherry tomatoes to a baking tray. Drizzle with 1 tbsp olive oil and a pinch of salt and give it a good mix up. Put the tray in the oven for 10-15 min or until everything is starting to brown

4) Add the chicken to the tray with the potatoes and return it to the oven for a further 10-15 min or until cooked through

5) While the chicken is cooking, boil a full kettle

6) Peel and finely slice (don't chop!) the garlic

7) Dissolve 1/2 chicken stock cube in 150ml [250ml] boiled water

8) Add the baby leaf spinach to a colander and pour the remaining boiled water all over the spinach until it starts to wilt

9) Rinse the wilted spinach under the cold tap until it's cool

10) Once cool, squeeze any excess water out of the spinach (as much as you can!)

11) Heat a large wide-based pan with 1/2 tbsp olive oil over a medium-low heat. Once hot, add the sliced garlic and cook for 2-3 min or until softened but not browned

12) Once softened, add 1 tsp flour and cook for 1 min

13) Add the chicken stock and cook for 2-3 min or until thickened

14) Once thickened, add the soft cheese and stir it all together – this is your creamy sauce

15) Add the wilted spinach and roasted cherry tomatoes to the creamy sauce and gently mix together – this is your creamy spinach & tomato sauce

16) Slice the cooked chicken finely. Serve the roast potatoes with the sliced chicken and creamy spinach & tomato sauce to the side. Finish with a grind of black pepper
