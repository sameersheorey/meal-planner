Tuna burgers

Fish

Prep Time: 15 Cook Time: 20 Total Time: 35 Servings: 2

Ingredients

2 cans of tuna

1 inch ginger, grated

4 spring onions, finely chopped

1 tbsp soy sauce

1 chilli, chopped

1 egg

Directions

1) Drain tuna and transfer to bowl.
2) Add ginger, spring onions, soy sauce, chilli and egg and mix to combine.
3) Shape into burger patties using can.
4) Cook burgers at 160C for 20 mins.
