Walnut & Cheddar Pesto Chicken Bulgur

Chicken, Dinner, Italian

Total Time: 30 min Servings: 2 Source: Gousto.co.uk

Ingredients

1 garlic clove

20g basil

40g mature cheddar

125g cherry tomatoes

30g walnuts

1 tbsp red wine vinegar

130g bulgur wheat

250g chicken breast fillets

Description

To whip up a delicious dinner in half an hour, you'll sear juicy British chicken with summer-ripe tomatoes, add wholesome bulgur and finish with a generous drizzle of walnut and cheddar pesto.

Directions

1) Boil a kettle

2) Add the bulgur wheat to a pot with plenty of boiled water and a generous pinch of salt. Cook over a high heat for 7-9 min or until tender with a slight bite, then drain and return to the pot.

3) Meanwhile, heat a large, wide-based pan over a medium heat. Once hot, add the walnuts and toast for 3-4 min, or until they've darkened in colour slightly

4) While the walnuts are toasting, chop the basil coarsely (including the stalks)

5) Peel and roughly chop the garlic

6) Break the cheddar into small pieces

7) Transfer the toasted walnuts to a food processor and reserve the pan for later.

8) Add the chopped basil, chopped garlic, cheddar pieces, red wine vinegar, a pinch of salt and pepper, and 3 tbsp [6 tbsp] olive oil. Blitz together to form a coarse purée – this is your walnut & cheddar pesto

9) Slice the chicken breasts into strips

10) Return the reserved pan to a medium heat with 1 tbsp [2 tbsp] olive oil. Add the sliced chicken with a pinch of salt and cook for 2-3 min on each side until golden

11) While the chicken is cooking, cut the cherry tomatoes in half

12) Once the chicken has browned, add the tomatoes and cook for a further 2 min, or until they've warmed through

13) Fluff the cooked bulgur with a fork

14) Stir the fluffed bulgur and half of the walnut & cheddar pesto (reserve the rest for garnish!) through the chicken and tomatoes, until everything is lightly coated in the pan juices and pesto – this is your walnut & cheddar pesto chicken bulgur

15) Serve the walnut & cheddar pesto chicken bulgur with the remaining pesto dotted over
