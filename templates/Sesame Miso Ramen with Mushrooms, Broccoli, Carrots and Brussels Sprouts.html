Sesame Miso Ramen with Mushrooms, Broccoli, Carrots and Brussels Sprouts

Japanese, Vegetarian

Prep Time: 10 minutes Cook Time: 20 minutes Servings: 2 Source: Oddbox.co.uk

Ingredients

200 g Mushrooms

100 g Broccoli (optional)

1 Carrot

200 g Brussels sprouts (optional)

300 ml Broth

1 tbsp Miso paste

1 tbsp Soy sauce

1 tbsp Rice vinegar

1 tbsp Ginger (grated)

2 Garlic cloves (grated)

120 g Noodles

1 tbsp Sesame seeds

a pinch of chilli flakes

Olive oil

Salt & pepper

Directions

1) In a large saucepan, combine the broth, 400 ml water, soy sauce, rice vinegar, miso, and chilli flakes. Add the ginger, and garlic. Simmer on low heat for 10 minutes.

2) Bring the broth to a boil and stir in the noodles, and broccoli. Cook for 5 minutes, or until the noodles are soft.

3) Preheat the oven to 190 C/170 fan/gas mark 5. Lay the Brussels sprouts and mushrooms on a large baking tray. Drizzle with olive oil, and season with salt and pepper.

4) Roast for 10-15 minutes, until cooked and golden.

5) Ladle the noodle broth into bowls and top with the crispy mushrooms, Brussels sprouts, grated carrot and sesame seeds.


- Store in an airtight container in the fridge for up to 3 days, or in the freezer for up to 1 month.
