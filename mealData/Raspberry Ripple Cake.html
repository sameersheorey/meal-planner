Raspberry Ripple Cake

Baking, Cakes

Prep Time: 45 mins Cook Time: 18-25 mins Total Time: 70 mins Servings: 12 Source: The Baking Club - BakedIn

Ingredients

195g butter (cubed at room temp)

205g granulated sugar (130g + 75g)

4 medium eggs

195g self-raising flour

1 tsp baking powder

120g raspberries

350g mascarpone

130g icing sugar

2.5g freeze dried raspberries (optional for decoration)

Directions

1) Preheat oven to 160C fan/gas mark 4

2) Grease and line three 18cm round tins

3) Using a wooden spoon, beat together butter and 130g sugar until light and fluffy

4) Add eggs, flour and baking powder and beat until the mixture is smooth.

5) Divide the mixture evenly between the three tins and smooth.

6) Bake for 18-25 minutes or until golden brown and well risen.

7) Leave to cool in the tins for a few minutes, then turn out onto a wire rack and leave to cool completely

8) While the cakes are baking, make the jam. Put raspberries, 75g granulated sugar and 70 ml water into a saucepan. Bring to the boil, then lower the heat and simmer for 20-25 minutes

9) When the jam has thickened slightly, remove from the heat and push the jam through a sieve with the back of a spoon. Discard the seeds and pulp. Leave the remaining jam syrup to cool completely

10) Whilst the cake is cooling, make the icing. Briefly beat the mascarpone in a bowl then gradually add the icing sugar and mix until well combined

11) Once the cake layers are cool, assemble the cake. Position the first sponge on a plate and spread 2 heaped tbsp of mascarpone icing onto the sponge.

12) Dollop a third of the jam syrup over the icing and using a skewer swirl the jam into the icing.

13) Repeat steps 11 and 12 with the second layer

14) Put the final sponge on top and spread a generous amount of icing over the top of the cake. Scrape a thin layer of icing around the sides of the cake with a flat knife.

15) Dollop the jam syrup over the top of the cake and swirl carefully to achieve a distinctive ripple effect

16) To finish decorate with freeze dried raspberries
