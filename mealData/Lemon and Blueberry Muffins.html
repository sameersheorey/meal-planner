Lemon and Blueberry Muffins

Baking, Cakes

Prep Time: less than 30 mins Cook Time: 10 to 30 mins Servings: 12 Source: Bbc.co.uk

Ingredients

100g butter

250g self-raising flour

1 tsp bicarbonate of soda

100g golden caster sugar (plus 2 tsp for sprinkling)

100g blueberries

1 lemon, zest only

2 free-range eggs

150ml low-fat natural yogurt

2 tbsp milk

12 tsp lemon curd

Directions

1) Preheat the oven to 200C/Gas 6. Line a 12-hole deep muffin tin with paper/silicon cases.

2) Melt the butter in the microwave then set aside to cool for a few minutes.

3) Sift the flour and bicarbonate of soda into a large bowl and stir in the sugar, blueberries and lemon zest. Make a well in the centre.

4) Beat the eggs with an electric whisk until smooth then beat in the yogurt, milk and melted butter until well combined.

5) Stir into the flour mixture with a large metal spoon until very lightly mixed.

6) Divide the batter between the muffin cases. Place a teaspoon of the lemon curd on top of each one and sprinkle with the remaining sugar.

7) Bake for 20 minutes or until well risen and golden-brown. Serve warm or allow to cool on a wire rack. (The lemon curd will be very hot, so don’t eat as soon as they come out of the oven.)
