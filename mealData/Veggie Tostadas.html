Veggie Tostadas

Dinner, Mexican, Vegetarian

Total Time: 20 min Servings: 2 Source: Hellofresh.co.uk

Ingredients

1 peppers

4 carrot

1 tin red kidney beans

2 tsp fajita mix

2 tin chopped tomatoes

1 vegetable stock cube

4 tortilla wraps

1/2 lime

1 bunch coriander

75g sour cream

Directions

1) Preheat your oven to 200°C.

2) Thinly slice the pepper and put on a baking tray, drizzle with oil and season with salt and pepper. Toss to coat, spread out and roast on the top shelf of your oven until soft and slightly charred, 12-15 mins, turning halfway.

3) Meanwhile, trim the carrot and grate on the coarse side of your grater (no need to peel).

4) Drain and rinse the kidney beans in a sieve.

5) Heat a drizzle of oil in a frying pan on medium-high heat and add the beans and Mexican spice. Cook, stirring, for 1 minute.

6) Stir in the grated carrot, finely chopped tomatoes and vegetable stock powder. Bring to the boil, stir to dissolve the stock and reduce the heat to medium and simmer, stirring occasionally, until thick and tomatoey, 7-8 mins.

7) Halfway through your peppers cooking time, lightly oil another baking tray and pop the tortillas onto the tray in a single layer. Drizzle on some oil and season with salt and pepper. Bake in the oven on the middle shelf until crisp and golden, 4-5 mins. TIP: Watch they don't burn!

8) Meanwhile, roughly chop the coriander (stalks and all).

9) Zest the lime and chop into wedges.

10) Pop the sour cream in a small bowl. Add the lime zest and season with salt and pepper. Stir together.

11) Stir half the coriander through the bean mixture and season to taste with salt and pepper if needed.

12) Serve the crisp tortillas on plates topped with a helping of the veggie bean mix, the roasted peppers, a dollop of lime crema and a sprinkling of remaining coriander. Finish with lime wedges for squeezing over.
