Pulled Hoisin Chicken & Sesame Noodles

Chicken, Chinese, Dinner

Prep Time: 25 min Servings: 2 Source: Gousto.co.uk

Ingredients

1 red chilli

2 garlic cloves

15g fresh root ginger

2 spring onions

320g chicken thighs

150g spring greens

2 wholewheat noodle nests

1 tbsp toasted sesame oil

2 tbsp hoisin sauce

2 tbsp soy sauce

5g toasted sesame seeds

Description

Get your wok sizzling! In this dish, you'll smother tender British chicken thighs with sweet, fiery hoisin sauce and serve them on wholewheat sesame noodles with garlicky greens. Hao chi (delicious)!

Directions

1) Heat a large, wide-based pan with a matching lid, with a drizzle of vegetable oil over a high heat. Once hot, add the chicken thighs with a generous pinch of both salt and pepper and cook for 3 min on each side or until they're browned.

2) Meanwhile, peel and finely chop the ginger and garlic. Cut a few chilli rounds then cut the remaining red chilli in half lengthways, deseed and chop ﬁnely

3) Boil a kettle

4) Once the chicken has browned, reduce the heat to medium-low and add a splash of water and cook, covered for 10 min or until the chicken is cooked through

5) While the chicken is cooking, add the wholewheat noodles to a pot and cover them with boiled water until they're fully submerged. Bring to the boil over a high heat and cook for 5-7 min or until tender with a slight bite, then drain and set aside in a bowl and reserve the pot for later

6) Rip the leaves off the spring greens, discard the tough stalks, then layer the leaves over each other, roll them up and slice into thin strips

7) Trim, then slice the spring onions finely

8) Once the chicken is cooked, remove the pan from the heat and shred the chicken apart with two forks. Once pulled, return the pan to a medium heat and add the chopped ginger, chopped chilli, hoisin sauce, half the soy sauce and 50ml [75ml] water and cook for 3-4 min or until the chicken is coated in a sticky sauce – this is your pulled hoisin chicken

9) Meanwhile, return the reserved pot to a medium heat with a drizzle of vegetable oil. Once hot, add the sliced spring greens, chopped garlic and a generous pinch of salt and cook for 1 min or until starting to wilt

10) Add a splash of water and cook, covered, for 2 min or until completely wilted, then stir in the cooked noodles, toasted sesame oil and remaining soy sauce – these are your sesame noodles

11) Serve the pulled hoisin chicken over the sesame noodles. Garnish with the toasted sesame seeds, chilli rounds and sliced spring onion
