Turkish-style Couscous

Lunch

Servings: 4

Ingredients

250 g couscous

1 tsp ground cumin

1 tsp smoked paprika

1 medium red onion

1 medium cucumber

1 carrot

2 ripe tomatoes

1 fresh red chilli

1 bunch of fresh mint leaves

1 bunch of fresh flat-leaf parsley

1 tbsp tomato purée

2 tbsp extra virgin olive oil

½ lemon, zest and juice (optional)

Directions

1) Place the couscous, cumin and paprika into a bowl. Stir to combine, then pour over just enough boiling water to cover the couscous. Cover the bowl and leave for about 10 minutes.

2) Meanwhile, finely chop the onion, cucumber, tomatoes carrots and chilli. Finely chop the mint leaves and the parsley (including the stalks).

3) Mix the couscous up with a fork, then stir in the tomato purée until well coated. Add all the chopped vegetables, chilli and herbs and mix well. Stir in the oil and the lemon zest and juice, then season to how you like it.
