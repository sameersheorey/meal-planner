Courgette, Lemon and Pistachio Cake

Cakes

Cook Time: 45-50

Ingredients

100g pistachios

300g courgettes

3 medium eggs

200g golden castor sugar

225g plain white flour

Finely grated zest of 2 lemons

2 tsp baking powder

1/2 tsp bicarb


To decorate:

125g icing sugar (sifted)

3 tbsp lemon juice

Roughly chopped pistachios

Directions

1) Preheat oven to 160 C fan.

2) Grind 100g pistachios to powder

3) Trim courgettes’ ends and grate them finely.

4) Beat the eggs and sugar until pale light and creamy.

5) Fold in courgettes, ground pistachios, flour, lemon zest, baking powder and bicarb.

6) Combine everything, scooping up the mixture from the bottom of the bowl but be careful not to over-mix.

7) Put into buttered and base lined 8″ cake tin.

8) Bake for 45-50 minutes. The cake is cooked when a skewer inserted into the centre comes out clean.

9) Leave to cool in the tin for 10 minutes then turn the cake onto a wire rack, peel off the paper and leave to cool completely, up the right way.

10) To make the icing, mix the icing sugar with the lemon juice until it is smooth. Place in fridge to firm up before spreading over the cake.

11) Leave to set a little before sprinkling with chopped pistachios.
