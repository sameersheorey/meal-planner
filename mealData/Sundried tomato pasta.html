Sundried tomato pasta

Lunch

Total Time: 20-30 Servings: 4 Source: Bbcgoodfood.com

Ingredients

300g pasta (farfalle)

200g frozen peas

1 large tomato

10 sundried tomatoes in oil

2 tbsp olive oil

2 tsp white wine vinegar

1 garlic clove

10 basil leaves

Mattesson's smoked sausage (optional)

Directions

1) Boil the pasta for 8 minutes, add the peas and cook for 2 minutes more until the pasta and peas are tender. Drain and cool

2) Roughly chop the tomato and put in a food processor with the sun-dried tomatoes, olive oil, 8 basil leaves, vinegar and garlic

3) Mix the pasta, peas, dressing, remaining basil leaves and sliced sausage (could use other meats, mozzarella or feta)
