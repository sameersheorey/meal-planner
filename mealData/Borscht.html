Borscht

★★★

Lunch, Regulars, Russian, Vegan, Vegetarian

Prep Time: 30 mins Cook Time: 1 hr Total Time: 1.5 hrs Servings: 4 Source: Hairy Bikers

Ingredients

1 tbsp vegetable oil

3 medium-sized beetroot (~450g), diced

1 large carrot, diced

1 stick of celery, diced

1 large waxy potato, diced

1 onion, finely chopped

1 can black beans or kidney beans

2 garlic cloves, finely chopped

1.5 litres stock

½ green cabbage, finely shredded

2 tomatoes, peeled and chopped

Flaked sea salt

Freshly ground black pepper

(Vegan) Soured cream or crème fraîche

Directions

1) Heat the oil in a large heavy-based saucepan. Add the beetroot, carrot, celery, potato, onion and garlic, then sauté for a couple of minutes until the veg are well combined and coated with the fat. Add the stock and drained beans and season with salt and black pepper.

2) Bring the soup almost to the boil, then cover and simmer for about 15 minutes.

3) Add the cabbage and tomatoes. Cover the pan and leave the soup to simmer for another 20 minutes. Taste for seasoning and add more salt and pepper if necessary.

4) Serve with a dollop of crème fraiche
