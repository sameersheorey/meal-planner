Slow-Cooker Turkey Chili

Chicken, Dinner

Prep Time: 20 mins Cook Time: 4 hours Total Time: 4 hours 20 mins Servings: 8 Source: Delish.com

Ingredients

1 tbsp oil

1 red onion

1 green pepper

750g turkey mince

2 cloves garlic, minced

2 tbsp. tomato puree

1 tin chopped tomatoes 

1 tin black beans, rinsed and drained

1 tin kidney beans, rinsed and drained

1 chicken stock cube

2 tsp. chili powder

1 tsp. ground cumin

1 tsp. dried oregano


Optional garnish:

Grated cheese

Spring onions

Directions

1) Dice the onion and pepper

2) Heat oil at medium-high heat in a large pan (or slow cooker pot)

3) Add onion and pepper and cook until beginning to soften, about 4 minutes

4) Add ground turkey and cook, stirring occasionally, until turkey is browned. Season with salt and pepper, then stir in garlic and tomato puree and cook until fragrant, about 2 minutes.

5) Transfer mixture to a slow cooker and add tomatoes, black beans, kidney beans, chicken broth, chili powder, cumin and oregano.

6) Cook on high for 4 hours, until chili has thickened. Check for seasoning and season with salt and pepper to taste.

7) Garnish with cheese and spring onions, if desired.
