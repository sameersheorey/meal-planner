Marrow and Ginger Chutney

Chutney

Ingredients

1.5kg prepared marrow

250g onion

3 cloves garlic

250g apple

300g brown sugar

600ml vinegar

175g sultanas

1/2 tsp cayenne pepper

1 tbsp grated root ginger

12 peppercorns in muslin bag

2 tbsp cornflour

Directions

Cube marrow and apple, chop onion and garlic.
Slowly bring to a simmer, stirring, until the sugar has dissolved. Cook until tender and thicker (1-2 hours)
Stir in dissolved cornflour until desired thickness is reached.
Pour into prepared jars.

Notes

Made 6-7 jars
