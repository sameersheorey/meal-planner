Kale Bulgar

Dinner, Vegetarian

Prep Time: 15 minutes Cook Time: 1 hour 15 minutes Servings: Serving: 1 Source: Minimalistbaker.com

Ingredients

135g bulgar wheat

2 tbsp vegetable oil

210g sliced mushrooms

1 tbsp soy sauce

2 cloves garlic, minced

135g chopped kale or spinach

60g roughly chopped walnuts (or other nut or seed of choice) (optional)

Directions

1) Prepare bulgur wheat by covering with 2-3 cm boiling water, covering and leaving to soak for 30 mins.

2) Once the bulgar wheat is cooked, heat a large frying pan over medium heat, once hot add oil and bulgar. Sauté for 5-8 minutes, stirring occasionally, until slightly crispy and browned. Season with half the soy sauce for flavor. Then remove from skillet and set aside.

3) To the still hot skillet add the mushrooms and the other half of the soy sauce. Sauté for 2-3 minutes, or until browned and reduced in size.

4) Then add garlic and kale and walnuts (optional) and sauté for another 1-2 minutes or until kale is just wilted.
