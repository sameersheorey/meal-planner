Crispy Baked Gnocchi with Tomatoes, Basil and Mozzarella

★★

Dinner, Italian, Vegetarian

Prep Time: 10 minutes Cook Time: 30 minutes Total Time: 40 min Servings: 2 Source: Food Republic

Ingredients

450g potato gnocchi

2 tablespoons olive oil

340g cherry tomatoes

1 ball fresh mozzarella

1 large bunch of fresh basil

45g pine nuts

Directions

For the gnocchi:

1)Preheat your oven to 220°C.

2) Place the gnocchi in a large bowl and cover with boiling water. Leave to cook for 2 minutes, then drain well.

3) Add the cooked gnocchi to a roasting pan or large baking dish along with the olive oil and mix well to evenly coat.

4) Chop the tomatoes in half, the mozzarella into chunks, and remove leaves from basil discarding the stalks

5) Tuck in the tomatoes, mozzarella, and half of the basil.

6) Season with sea salt and freshly ground black pepper, then transfer to the oven and bake for 20 minutes.

7) Scatter the pine nuts over the gnocchi and bake for a further 5 minutes.

8) Tear the rest of the basil leaves, scatter them over the gnocchi, and serve immediately.
