Brazilian Black Beans & Zesty Lime Chicken

★★★★★

Brazilian, Chicken, Dinner, Regulars

Total Time: 30 min Servings: 2 Source: Gousto.co.uk

Ingredients

1 brown onion

130g brown rice

1 red chilli

1 lime

2 garlic cloves

1 spring onion

1 large chicken breast fillet

1tbso mango chutney

2 tsp smoked paprika

1/2 tsp ground cinnamon

5g coriander

1 can of black beans

1 chicken stock cube

1 tbsp chipotle paste

Description

Black beans, smoky chipotle paste and a touch of cinnamon come together in perfect harmony in this delicious Brazilian-style stew. After mashing the beans, you’ll top this flavoursome dish with mango and chilli flavoured shredded chicken, coriander and a squeeze of lime

Directions

1) Rinse the brown rice, add it to a pot with plenty of cold water and bring to the boil over a high heat. Once boiling, reduce the heat to medium and cook for 20-25 min or until it's tender with a slight bite. Once cooked, drain and return it to the pot and keep covered until serving. 

2) While the rice is cooking, peel and finely chop the brown onion.

3) Heat a second pot with a drizzle of vegetable oil and a large knob of butter over a very low heat. Once hot, add the chopped onion with a pinch of salt and cook for 6-7 min or until softened

4) Boil half a kettle 

5) Heat a large, wide-based pan with a matching lid over a medium-high heat. Once hot, add the chicken breast with a generous pinch of salt and cook for 3 min on each side or until golden

6) Add a couple of splashes of water to the pan and cover with a lid. Cook for a further 10-15 min or until the chicken is cooked through

7) Meanwhile, peel and finely chop the garlic 

8) Dissolve the chicken stock cube and chipotle paste in 200ml [320ml] boiled water 

9) Drain and rinse the black beans

10) Once the onion has softened, increase the heat to medium, add the chopped garlic, smoked paprika and ground cinnamon to the pan and cook for 30 sec or until fragrant

11) Add the drained black beans and chicken stock and cook for 7 min 

12) While the black beans are cooking, pick the coriander leaves from their stems, discarding the stems 

13) Trim, then slice the spring onion finely

14) Cut the red chilli in half lengthways, deseed and chop ﬁnely

15) Once done, add 30g [60g] butter to the beans and mash them until smooth – these are your Brazilian black beans 

16) Once done, transfer the chicken to a clean board and shred it apart, using two forks. Return the shredded chicken to the pan with the mango chutney, chopped spring onion and chopped chilli. Zest and juice 1/2 [1] lime into the pan and give everything a good mix up – this is your limey chicken

17) Cut the remaining lime into wedges 

18) Serve the Brazilian black beans over the cooked brown rice and top with the limey chicken. Garnish with the coriander leaves and lime wedges
