Coffee and Walnut Cake

Cakes

Cook Time: 20-25 Servings: 2 sandwich cakes

Ingredients

10oz Self raising flour


2.5 level teaspoons baking powder

10oz soft light brown sugar

10oz soft creaming margarine

2.5 teaspoons coffee essence *

5 Eggs

2.5oz Chopped walnuts


For the frosting (two cakes):

14oz icing sugar

3oz butter

4 tablespoons water

2oz castor sugar

4 teaspoons coffee essence


*Substitute 1 tsp instant coffee dissolved in 1 tsp boiling water for each teaspoon essence.

Directions

1) Preheat oven to 160˚C (fan) or gas 4.

2) Grease 4 7” sponge tins and line with a circle of greaseproof paper.

3) Sift the flour and baking powder into a mixing bowl.

4) Add the margarine, brown sugar, coffee essence, eggs and walnuts.

5) Using a wooden spoon mix first to blend ingredients, then beat for 1 minute to get a smooth cake batter.

6) Divide the mixture equally between the prepared tins and spread level.

7) Place in the centre of the preheated oven and bake for 25 minutes. (Fan oven – check after 20 mins.)

8) Let cake layers cool in the tin for 2 minutes then turn out and leave until cold.


9) Meanwhile sift the icing sugar for the frosting into a mixing bowl.

10) Measure butter, water, sugar and coffee essence into a saucepan. Stir over low heat until the sugar has dissolved and butter melted. Bring just to the boil and pour at once into the sifted ingredients.

11) Beat well to make a smooth glossy icing.

12) Set aside until quite cold then beat again to make a soft fudge icing.

13) Sandwich cake layers with half the frosting mixture.

14) Spoon remainder on top and spread level, then rough up the surface with the tip of a knife.
