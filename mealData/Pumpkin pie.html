Pumpkin pie

Source: Allrecipes.com

Ingredients

210g plain flour

pinch salt

105g butter, cubed

2–3 tbsp cold water

425g pumpkin puree

1 can sweetened condensed milk

2 large eggs

1 tsp ground cinnamon

½ tsp ground ginger

½ tsp ground nutmeg

½ tsp salt

Directions

1) Put the flour, salt and butter in the food processor and pulse until the fat is rubbed into the flour.

2) With the motor running, gradually add the water through the funnel until the dough comes together. Only add enough water to bind it and then stop.

3) Roll out pastry to line the tin, rest the pastry case in the fridge for 30 minutes

4) Preheat oven to 220C

5) Blind bake crust for 12 minutes

5) Whisk pumpkin puree, condensed milk, eggs, cinnamon, ginger, nutmeg, and salt together until smooth

6) Pour filling into pastry case

7) Bake for 15 minutes, reduce temperature to 175C and bake for further 35 minutes
