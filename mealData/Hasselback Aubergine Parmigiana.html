Hasselback Aubergine Parmigiana

Prep Time: 15 minutes Cook Time: 40 minutes Servings: 2 Source: Oddbox.co.uk

Ingredients

1 Aubergines

1 Onion

1 Mozzarella

30 g Parmesan

10 Basil leaves

400 g Passata

400 g Tin chopped tomatoes

1 tbsp Oregano

Olive oil

Salt & pepper

Directions

1) Make widthways cuts across the aubergines at 1cm intervals, three quarters of the way through (make sure the slices are still attached at the bottom). Top tip: Use two wooden spoons on each side of the aubergine to get perfect hasselback cuts and avoid cutting through.

2) Cook aubergine in airfryer for 10 mins at 180C

3) Prepare the tomato sauce by dicing the onion.

4) Heat 1 tbsp olive oil in a large frying pan over a medium heat and fry the onion for 5 minutes to soften. Add the oregano, tomato sauce and chopped tomatoes. Season with salt and pepper, then simmer for 10 minutes.

5) Slice the mozzarella, grate the parmesan and pick the basil leaves and gently stuff it all into the aubergine cuts.

6) Place the stuffed aubergines in the baking dish pour over the sauce. Drizzle with a little olive oil, season to taste and bake for 35-45 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and the aubergines are tender. Top with more fresh basil and serve.

- Store the hasselback aubergine parmigiana in an airtight container, in the fridge, for up to 3 days.
