Gambian style mushroom and sweet potato peanut stew

African, Vegan, Vegetarian

Ingredients

360g Fable meaty pulled

2 brown onions

2 tbsp curry powder

2 tsp ground paprika

2 red chillies

60g peanut butter

2 sweet potatoes

2 tbsp flour

650ml water (for stock)

4 tbsp tomato puree

2 vegetable stock cubes

260g basmati rice

Directions

1) Peel and chop onions into wedges

2) Chop sweet potato (skin on) into bite-sized pieces

3) Slice red chillies finely

4) Preheat oven to 180C

5) Heat an hob-safe, oven-proof casserole dish with drizzle of vegetable oil over a high heat. Once hot add Fable mushrooms and cook for 2-3 mins

6)Add onion, sweet potato and half chilli, curry powder, paprika and flour to dish and mix up.

7) Boil kettle, add boiled water, tomato puree, peanut butter and stock cubes. Bring to boil and season with grind of black pepper.

8) Cover with lid and put in oven for 35 mins

9) Reboil kettle, put rice and water in oven-proof dish with lid and place in oven for 25-30 mins

10) Serve stew over the rice and add remaining chili
