Paprika Chicken With Tomato & Coconut Dal

Chicken, Dinner, Indian

Prep Time: 40 min Servings: 2 Source: Gousto.co.uk

Ingredients

1 brown onion

100g red lentils

2 garlic cloves

1 large chicken breast fillet

1 tsp nigella seeds

1 tsp ground turmeric

1 tbsp curry powder

125g baby plum tomatoes

5g coriander

2 tsp ground paprika

2 mini coriander naans

1 chicken stock cube

2 tbsp cider vinegar

50g solid coconut cream

Description

This indulgent dal combines lentils with a tomato and coconut base, succulent smoky chicken and coriander naan for a mouthwatering feast.

Directions

1) Preheat the oven to 180°C (fan)/Gas 6

2) Boil a kettle

3) Peel and finely slice brown onion and garlic

4) Heat a large, wide-based pan with a drizzle of vegetable oil over a medium heat. Once hot, add the sliced onion with a pinch of salt and cook for 5-7 min or until starting to soften 

5) Meanwhile, roughly chop the coconut cream roughly and dissolve in 750ml [1.3L] boiled water with the chicken stock cube and a large pinch of sugar – this is your coconut stock

6) Once the onion has softened, add the ground turmeric, curry powder and chopped garlic and cook for 1-2 min further or until fragrant

7) Once fragrant, add the red lentils to the pan with the coconut stock. Reduce the heat to medium-low and cook for 25-30 min or until the lentils are almost cooked and the sauce has thickened 

8) While the lentils are cooking, dice the chicken into small bite-sized pieces and add to a bowl with the paprika, nigella seeds and a generous pinch of salt and give everything a good mix up

9) Chop the coriander finely (including the stalks)

10) Chop the baby plum tomatoes in half

11) Add the tomato halves and cook for 5-7 min further or until the tomatoes have softened and the lentils are fully cooked

12) Stir most of the chopped coriander (save some for garnish) through the lentils – this is your tomato & coconut dal

13) Heat a separate large, wide-based pan with a drizzle of vegetable oil over a high heat. Once hot, add the chicken and cook for 3-4 min

14) Add the cider vinegar and cook for further 2-3 min or until the chicken is cooked through

15) Meanwhile, add the mini coriander naans to a baking tray and put the tray in the oven for 3-4 min or until warmed through

16) Serve the paprika chicken over the tomato & coconut dal with the mini coriander naan to the side
