Spinach Egg Bake

Breakfast

Prep Time: 15 mins Cook Time: 15 mins Servings: 4 Source: Frontiercoop.com

Ingredients

12 eggs

180 ml whole milk

1 tsp rosemary

1 tsp thyme

120g spinach

45g mature cheddar

Directions

1) Preheat oven to 190˚C

2) Crack eggs into a large bowl and add the milk, herbs, and black pepper. Whisk well to combine.

3) Divide the spinach across 8 sections of a moulded tin.

4) Divide the egg mixture evenly over the spinach, filling each cup about ¾ of the way full.

5) Sprinkle grated cheese over each egg/spinach mixture.

6) Place the pan in the oven and bake for 30 to 35 minutes. Eggs should looked puffed and starting to brown on the top. If the egg still looks wet, return to the oven and continue cooking for another two minutes.
