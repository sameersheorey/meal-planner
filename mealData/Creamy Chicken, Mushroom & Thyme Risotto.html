Creamy Chicken, Mushroom & Thyme Risotto

Chicken, Dinner, Italian

Total Time: 35 min Servings: 2 Source: Gousto.co.uk

Ingredients

1 brown onion

160g arborio rice

2 garlic cloves

5g thyme

40g cheddar cheese

1 chicken stock cube

50g soft cheese

160g chestnut mushrooms

250g chicken breast fillets

Description

Relax and enjoy a luxurious one-pot, filled with caramelised onion and mushrooms. You’ll also add garlic and thyme, then top it with cheese for extra indulgence. Buon appetito!

Directions

1) Cut the chicken breasts into bite-sized pieces

2) Heat a large, wide-based pan with a drizzle of olive oil over a medium-high heat. Once hot, add the chopped chicken and cook for 3 min on each side or until browned

3) While the chicken is cooking, peel and finely dice the brown onion

4) Chop the chestnut mushrooms in half

5) Once the chicken has browned, transfer it to a plate and set aside until later

6) Return the pan to a medium heat with a drizzle of olive oil. Once hot, add the chopped onion and halved mushrooms and cook for 7-8 min or until softened

7) Meanwhile, boil a kettle

8) Strip the thyme leaves and chop them finely, discard the stems

9) Peel and finely chop the garlic

10) Dissolve the chicken stock cube in 700ml [1.4L] boiled water

11) Once the vegetables have softened, add the chopped garlic, thyme and arborio rice to the pan and cook for 1-2 min

12) Add the chicken stock a ladle at a time, stirring continuously for 15-20 min or until all the stock is absorbed and the rice is cooked

13) While the rice is cooking, grate the cheddar cheese

14) Once the rice is cooked, return the reserved browned chicken to the pan, stir in the soft cheese with half of the cheddar and cook for 3-4 min further or until the chicken is cooked through – this is your chicken, mushroom & thyme risotto

15) Serve the chicken, mushroom & thyme risotto and sprinkle over the remaining grated cheese. Season with a generous grind of black pepper
