Smoky Fish Curry & Green Bean Rice

Dinner, Fish, Indian

Prep Time: 20 min Servings: 2 Source: Gousto.co.uk

Ingredients

1 brown onion

3 garlic cloves

40g Cornish clotted cream

1/2 tsp dried chilli flakes

1 tbsp curry powder

80g trimmed fine green beans

200g smoked basa

130g basmati rice

1tbsp tomato paste

1 tsp black mustard seeds

Description

For this fabulously simple fish curry, you'll simmer smoked fish with caramelised onion, spices and clotted cream. Serve over green bean rice for a luxurious curry, ready in a hurry!

Directions

1) Chop the green beans into bite-sized pieces

2) Add the basmati rice and 300ml [600ml] water to a pot with a lid and bring to the boil over a high heat. Once the water is boiling, reduce the heat to low and add the chopped green beans to the pot. Cover and cook for 10-15 min or until the rice and green beans are cooked. Once cooked, remove from the heat and keep covered until serving – this is your green bean rice

3) Boil a kettle

4) Peel and finely chop the brown onion and garlic

5) Heat a large, wide-based pan with a matching lid, with a drizzle of vegetable oil and a knob of butter over a medium heat. Once hot, add the chopped onion with a generous pinch of salt, cover with a lid and cook for 5 min or until soft

6) Meanwhile, chop the smoked basa into bite-sized pieces

7) Dissolve the tomato paste in 300ml [600ml] boiled water – this is your tomato stock

8) Once the onions are softened, add the chopped garlic, chilli flakes, black mustard seeds and curry powder and cook for 2 min

9) Add the tomato stock and clotted cream and cook for a further 2 min

10) Add the chopped basa to the pan, cover with a lid and cook for 4-5 min or until the basa is fully cooked – this is your fish curry

11) Season the fish curry with salt and pepper, serve the fish curry with the green bean rice
