Coconut and Peanut Aubergine Curry

Dinner, Thai, Vegetarian

Cook Time: 30 minutes Servings: 4 Source: Olivemagazine.com

Ingredients

1 tbsp oil

2 aubergines

2 onions

2 garlic cloves

1 frozen ginger cube

1 tsp cumin seeds

1 tsp coriander seeds

1 tsp turmeric

1/2 tsp chilli powder

400ml half-fat coconut milk

1 tbsp tamarind paste

1 tbsp peanut butter

Directions

1) Chop aubergine into large chunks

2) Heat the oil in a pan and cook the aubergine in batches until golden and soft. Transfer to a plate once cooked

3) Dice the onion

4) Add the onion to the same pan and cook until soft and golden.

5) Add the crushed garlic and ginger and cook for a minute.

6) Add the spices and cook for 2 minutes.

7) Tip in the coconut milk, tamarind and peanut butter. Simmer gently until the peanut butter dissolves.

8) Add the aubergine back and simmer for 15 minutes.

9) Stir through some coriander and serve with bread or rice.
