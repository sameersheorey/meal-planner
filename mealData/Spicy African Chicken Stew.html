Spicy African Chicken Stew

African, Chicken

Servings: 6 Source: BBC Good Food

Ingredients

1 chicken stock cube

150g smooth peanut butter

2 onions (chopped)

3 tbsp sunflower oil

3 tbsp finely chopped ginger

1 tsp cayenne pepper

3 tsp ground coriander

3 tsp ground cumin

2 chillies (chopped)

2 bay leaf

400g can chopped tomato

1 kg chicken thighs

2 medium sweet potatoes (chunks)

2 red peppers (chunks)

Description

Cayenne pepper and Scotch bonnet chillies give this boldly flavoured peanut casserole a fiery kick

Directions

1) Pour the hot stock over the peanut butter and stir until dissolved.

2) Fry the onions in the oil for 5 mins to soften, add the ginger, cayenne, ½ tsp black pepper, coriander, cumin, chillies and bay and cook, stirring, for 2 mins.

3) Transfer to slow cooker pot. Add the tomatoes, peanut stock and chicken and stir to mix.

4) Cook on low for about 8 hours.

5) Stir in the sweet potatoes and peppers about two hours before serving.
