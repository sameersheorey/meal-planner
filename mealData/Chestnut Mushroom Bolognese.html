Chestnut Mushroom Bolognese

Dinner, Italian, Vegetarian

Total Time: 40 min Servings: 2 Source: Gousto.co.uk

Ingredients

1 brown onion

3 garlic cloves

10g basil

1 carrot

1 vegetable stock cube

1 tbsp balsamic vinegar

190g linguine

80g baby button mushrooms

1 tbsp soy sauce

160g chestnut mushrooms

1 tbsp Worcestershire sauce (replace with Henderson's relish to make vegetarian)

40g cheddar cheese

2 tbsp tomato paste

Description

For this veggie bolognese, you'll make a flavour-packed sauce that nods to what bolognese is all about: easy, familiar comfort food. Top with basil and cheese, and it's ready. Bologn-easy!

Directions

1) Boil a kettle

2) Peel and finely dice the carrot and brown onion

3) Heat a large, wide-based pan with 2 tbsp [3 tbsp] vegetable oil over a medium-high heat. Once hot, add the diced carrot and diced onion with a large pinch of salt. Cook for 5 min or until starting to soften and caramelise

4) Meanwhile, bring a large pot of boiled water with a pinch of salt to the boil. Add the linguine, and cook for 8-10 min or until cooked through but with a slight bite. Once cooked, drain the linguine and reserve approx. 150ml [250ml] starchy cooking water for later

5) Meanwhile, grate half of the chestnut and button mushrooms and slice the rest

6) Peel and finely chop the garlic

7) Add both the grated and sliced mushrooms and chopped garlic to the carrot mixture and cook for 5 min

8) Crumble the vegetable stock cube into the softened mushroom mixture

9) Add the tomato paste, stir for 30 secs, then add the soy sauce, balsamic vinegar and Worcestershire sauce/Henderson's Relish

10) Add 1/2 tsp [1 tsp] sugar and season generously with pepper. Stir constantly until the mixture is evenly coated

11) Add the reserved starchy cooking water, a little at a time, stirring it in as you go

12) Reduce the heat to medium and cook until the sauce has the consistency of bolognese – this is your chestnut mushroom bolognese

Tip: Pasta water adds a little starch to the sauce, helping it stick to the linguine easier

13) Meanwhile, chop the basil finely including the stalks

14) Grate the cheddar cheese

15) Stir the drained linguine through the chestnut mushroom bolognese. Garnish with the chopped basil and grated cheese
