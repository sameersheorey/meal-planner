Kung Pao Cauliflower (and chicken)

Chicken, Chinese, Dinner, Vegetarian

Prep Time: 15 mins Cook Time: 30 mins Servings: Yield: 2 Source: Howsweeteats.com

Ingredients

1 cauliflower - cut into florets

Chicken breast (optional)

olive oil

sea salt

freshly cracked black pepper

2 tbsp soy sauce

2 tbsp hoisin sauce

2 tbsp red wine vinegar

1 tbsp toasted sesame oil

2 tsp brown sugar

2 tsp cornflour

2 garlic cloves, minced

2 tsp freshly grated ginger

1 tsp chilli flakes

50g peanuts, chopped

4 spring onions, thinly sliced

jasmine rice, for serving

Directions

1) Preheat the oven to 220 C

2) Cook the rice

3) Place the cauliflower on a baking tray and spritz it with olive oil. Sprinkle it with a pinch of salt and pepper. Roast the cauliflower for 15 minutes, until just slightly crisp.

4) If using, brown the chicken in a pan

5) While the cauliflower is roasting, whisk together the soy sauce, hoisin, vinegar, sesame oil, brown sugar, chilli flakes, cornflour, garlic and ginger.

6) Add the chicken to the tray and pour the sauce over the chicken and cauliflower. Toss well to coat all the cauliflower. Return the pan to the oven. Roast for another 15 to 20 minutes, stirring and tossing every 5 to 10 minutes.

7) Once cooked cover with the green onions and peanuts
