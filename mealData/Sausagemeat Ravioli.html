Sausagemeat Ravioli

Italian, Pork

Servings: 2

Ingredients

2 large eggs

200 g tipo '00' flour

1 chopped brown onion?

100 g sausagemeat?

1 tsp paprika?

10 g parmesan?

Directions

1) Put everything in the food processer and whiz until the flour looks like breadcrumbs.

2) Tip the mixture on to your work surface and bring the dough together into one lump, using your hands.

3) Knead and work the dough with your hands to develop the gluten in the flour. You’ll know when to stop – when your pasta starts to feel smooth and silky instead of rough and floury.

4) Wrap it in cling film and put it in the fridge to rest for at least half an hour before you use it. Make sure the cling film covers it well to prevent it drying out

5) While the dough is resting make the filling. Fry finely chopped onions and sausagemeat, season with pepper and paprika. When cooked add grated parmesan cook gently until melted. Remove from heat and blot excess oil with paper towel

6) Roll out dough using pasta maker until you can clearly see your hand through it (7 or lower)

7) Put pasta sheet into ravioli mould and fill with a small tsp of filling. Sandwich another pasta sheet on top. Use rolling pin to seal the edges, trying to expel all the air as you go.

8) Cook ravioli in a large pan of gently boiling salted water for 4-5 mins. Do not use a full rolling boil as it is likely to make ravioli split
