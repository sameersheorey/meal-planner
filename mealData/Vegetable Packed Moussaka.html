Vegetable Packed Moussaka

Total Time: 40 min Servings: 2 Source: Hellofresh.co.uk

Ingredients

1 aubergine

1 onion

1 courgette

1 yellow pepper

2 garlic cloves

2 tbsp tomato puree

1 cinnamon stick (or ½ tsp

ground cinnamon)

2 tsp oregano leaves (or dried oregano)

50g red split lentils

1 tbsp Worcestershire sauce

1 vegetable stock cube

1 tin chopped tomatoes

100ml crème fraiche

40g grated hard Italian cheese

Directions

1) Preheat your grill to high.

2) Slice the aubergine into 1cm thick slices.

3) Cut the onion in half through the root, peel and chop into ½cm pieces.

4) Cut the top and bottom off the courgette, cut into quarters lengthways, then chop into ½cm chunks.

5) Remove the core from the yellow pepper and chop into 1cm pieces.

6) Cut one one garlic clove in half (don't worry about peeling it). Pull the leaves from the oregano stalks and finely chop.

7) Put your aubergine slices on a baking tray in a single layer and drizzle over some oil, a pinch of salt and a good grind of black pepper. Put under your grill for 8-9 mins on each side, until they are nicely browned and soft. If they aren't soft in this time, just leave them under your grill for a few more mins. Remove and keep to the side when ready.

8) Whilst your aubergine cooks, make your tomato sauce! Put a frying pan on medium heat with a drizzle of oil and add your onion, cook for 5 mins until soft.

9) Add your courgette and pepper along with a good pinch of salt and a grind of black pepper. Cook for another 5 mins.

10) When your veggies are soft, add the tomato purée along with the cinnamon, grated garlic and oregano, cook for 1 minute more.

11) Add the lentils, Worcestershire sauce, vegetable stock and chopped tomatoes. Refill the tin(s) a third with water and pour this in too.

12) Simmer for 15-20 mins or until your lentils are soft. If it gets too dry, just add a splash of water.

13) When your tomato sauce is ready, transfer it to an ovenproof dish and layer your aubergine slices on top. Spoon over the crème fraîche and spread it out across the top. Sprinkle over the hard Italian cheese and add a grind of black pepper.

14) Put your moussaka under your grill for 5 mins, or until your cheese is golden and bubbling.
