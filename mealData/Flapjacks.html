Flapjacks

Baking, Biscuits

Prep Time: less than 30 mins Cook Time: 10 to 30 mins Servings: 12 Source: Bbc.co.uk

Ingredients

100g butter

100g golden syrup

50g mixed seeds, such as pumpkin, sunflower, linseed and sesame

100g raisins

50g dried apricots, cut into sixths

250g jumbo porridge oats

1 large free-range egg, beaten

Directions

1) Preheat the oven to 180C

2) Melt the butter and syrup together in microwave

3) Add the seeds, raisins, apricots and oats to a bowl add the melted butter and syrup and the egg.

4) Mix together

5) Spoon into flapjack mould and press until well flattened.

6) Bake for 18–20 minutes, or until golden and lightly browned around the edges.

Nutrition

Each serving provides 231 kcal, 4.5g protein, 27g carbohydrates (of which 14g sugars), 11g fat (of which 5g saturates), 3g fibre and 0.25g salt.
