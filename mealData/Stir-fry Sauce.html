Stir-fry Sauce

Dinner, Sauce

Servings: 2

Ingredients

2 small fresh red chillies

2-3 cloves garlic

2cm chunk fresh root ginger, chopped

1 tbsp honey or sugar

2 tbsp soy sauce

juice of 1/4 lemon

Directions

1) Combine all sauce ingredients until smooth in an electric blender.
2) Cook strips of meat in a wok for 1-2 minutes.
3) Add vegetables, then the sauce.
4) Continue to stir fry for 2-3 minutes until the vegetables are tender but still firm and everything is lightly dressed with the sauce.
5) Scatter with fresh coriander and sesame seeds or cashew nuts.
6) Serve immediately with rice.
