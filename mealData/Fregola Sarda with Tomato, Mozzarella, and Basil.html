Fregola Sarda with Tomato, Mozzarella, and Basil

Dinner, Italian, Seafood, Vegetarian

Servings: 2 Source: Food52.com

Ingredients

100g fregola sarda

1 medium shallot

1 garlic clove

2 tbsp flat leaf parsley

400g tinned tomatoes

250ml chicken stock

1 tsp sea salt

1 ball mozzarella

1 handful fresh basil

2 tbsp extra virgin olive oil

2 tbsp parmigiano reggiano

Description

Adaptation of recipe from Food52.com, still playing with quantities
Serve with seafood for a bigger meal

Directions

1) Finely mince the shallot, garlic, and parsley

2) In a wide bottomed pan with a lid, heat the olive oil and add the shallot, garlic, and half of the parsley.

3) Sweat over medium heat for 6-8 minutes or until the shallot and garlic start to give off aroma and are nicely sweated.

4) Add in the tomato and let this come to a low simmer

5) Next add in the chicken stock or water

6) Once this comes back to a simmer, add the fregola to the skillet, and stir to combine. Cover the skillet, and reduce heat to low, let the fregola simmer in the tomato until it is chewy but slightly al dente.

7) When the fregola is cooked, most of the liquid should be absorbed. Add in the remaining parsley, remove from heat, and stir in the mozzarella.

8) Transfer to a serving bowl and add in the torn basil leaves, a drizzle of Extra Virgin olive oil, and a few turns of freshly ground black pepper. Serve with parmigiano reggiano.
