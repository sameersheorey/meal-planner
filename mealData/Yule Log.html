Yule Log

Christmas

Cook Time: 15

Ingredients

3 eggs

75g caster sugar

65g flour

1 tsp baking powder

1 tbsp cocoa powder

150 ml filling (e.g. chocolate buttercream)

Directions

1) Whisk eggs and sugar together with electric whisk until very thick and beater leaves a heavy trail when lifted

2) Sift flour, baking powder and cocoa powder together twice, then fold lightly and evenly into the beaten mixture

3) Pour into a greased and lined Swiss roll tin (30x23cm) spreading right into corners

4) Bake in oven (preheated to 180C) for 10-15 minutes until just firm to the touch

5) Turn out onto greaseproof paper sprinkled with caster sugar

6) Trim edges to neaten and roll up whilst still warm (secure with loose elastic bands)

7) Once cool un-roll cake, spread with filling of choice (usually chocolate buttercream), and re-roll

8) Cover cake with chocolate icing (if desired), mark like a log with a fork, and sprinkle with icing sugar
