Spinach Lasagne

Dinner, Italian, Vegetarian

Prep Time: 15 Total Time: 1 HOUR 30 MINS Servings: 8 - 10 Source: Delish.com

Ingredients

1 box lasagne sheets

1 large onion, chopped

3 cloves garlic, crushed

1 tsp. dried oregano

2 cans tomatoes

10 mushrooms

2 peppers

4 carrots

x spinach, (can use frozen spinach that's been thawed and drained of excess liquid)

x g cheddar (grated)

500ml milk

1/2 tsp. ground cinnamon

x parmesan

350 g mozzarella (optional)

Directions

1) Preheat oven to 180ºC.

2) Cook lasagne sheets until al dente then drain.

3) In a large skillet over medium heat, heat oil. Add onion and garlic and season with salt, pepper, and oregano.

4) Add tomatoes, mushrooms, peppers and carrots and simmer for 20 mins

5) Add spinach and simmer until wilted

6) Add milk to jug, mix cornflour with a little water and add to milk.

7) Microwave until sauce has thickened then add grated cheddar and cinnamon and stir until cheese has melted into sauce

8) In a large baking dish, layer 5 lasagna sheets so they’re slightly overlapping. Spoon over a thin layer of the tomato sauce and top with a layer of cheese sauce. Sprinkle with a layer of parmesan/mozzarella. Repeat, ending with parmesan/mozzarella.

9) Cover with foil and bake 45 minutes, then remove foil and continue baking 15 minutes more.
