Portobello Mushroom Rogan Josh

Indian, Vegetarian

Prep Time: 30 min Total Time: 30 min Servings: 2 Source: Gousto.co.uk

Ingredients

1 brown onion

1 red chilli

2 tomatoes

10g coriander

1 tbsp mango chutney

1 tsp ground cumin

1 tsp ground coriander

130g basmati rice

1 vegetable stock cube

15g flaked almonds

80g baby button mushrooms

150g portobello mushrooms

1/2 tsp ground turmeric

1 tbsp tomato paste

Description

For this plant-based twist on a curry house favourite, you'll pack your sauce with spices, tomatoes and a hint of mango chutney. Serve with coriander rice, roasted portobellos and flaked almonds. Yum!

Directions

1) Preheat the oven to 180°C (fan)/Gas 6

2) Peel and finely chop the brown onion

3) Heat a large, wide-based pan with a matching lid, with a drizzle of vegetable oil over a medium-low heat. Once hot, add the chopped onion with a pinch of salt and sugar and cook for 8-10 min or until softened

4) Boil a kettle

5) Cut the red chilli in half lengthways, deseed and chop finely

6) Slice the portobello mushrooms. Cut the baby button mushrooms in half

7) Cut the tomatoes into wedges

8) Once the onion has softened, add the chopped chilli, ground coriander, ground turmeric and ground cumin to the pan. Increase the heat to medium and cook for 2-3 min or until fragrant

9) Meanwhile, dissolve the vegetable stock cube and mango chutney in 100ml [200ml] boiled water - this is your vegetable stock

10) Once fragrant, add the tomato paste and tomatoes and cook for 1 min

11) Add the baby button mushrooms and mix until fully coated in the spices. Once coated, add the vegetable stock and cook, covered, for 15-20 min or until the sauce has thickened - this is your mushroom rogan josh

12) Add the basmati rice and 300ml [600ml] cold water to a pot with a lid and bring to the boil over a high heat. Once boiling, reduce the heat to very low and cook, covered, for 10-12 min or until all the water has absorbed and the rice is cooked.

13) While the rice is cooking, add the sliced portobello mushrooms to a tray and put it in the oven for 10-12 min or until golden

14) Chop the coriander finely, including the stalks

15) Once cooked, fluff the rice with a fork and stir through the chopped coriander – this is your coriander rice

16) Serve the mushroom rogan josh with the coriander rice to the side. Garnish with the flaked almonds and roasted portobello mushroom slices
