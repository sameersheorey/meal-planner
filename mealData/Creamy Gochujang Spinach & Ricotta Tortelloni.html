Creamy Gochujang Spinach & Ricotta Tortelloni

★★★★

Dinner, Italian, Korean, Vegetarian, Weekend lunches

Ingredients

6 medium mushrooms

2 garlic cloves

1 tbsp gochujang paste

1 vegetable stock cube

300g spinach and ricotta tortelloni

75g creme fraiche

20g butter

20g parmesan

crispy onions for serving


Directions

1) Slice the mushrooms

2) Boil a kettle for the tortelloni

3) Heat a drizzle of oil in a large frying pan on high heat. Once hot, add the mushrooms, season with salt and pepper and fry, stirring occasionally, until browned, 5-6 mins.

4) Peel and grate the garlic (or use a garlic press).

5) Add the gochujang paste and garlic to the mushrooms. Stir-fry for 30 secs.

6) Stir in the vegetable stock cube and 100ml water for the sauce. Bring to the boil, then lower the heat and simmer until the liquid has reduced slightly, 2-3 mins.

7) Meanwhile, pour boiled water into saucepan with ½ tsp salt on high heat. Add the ravioli and bring to the boil. Cook until tender, 3 mins.

8) Once cooked, drain in a colander and pop back in the pan. Drizzle with oil and stir through to stop it sticking together.

9) Once the sauce has reduced, stir the creme fraiche, butter and half the parmesan into the pan. Simmer for 1 min. Add a splash of water if it's a little too thick, then remove from the heat.

10) Add the cooked ravioli to the sauce and stir gently to combine

11) Serve with the remaining parmesan and crispy onions.
