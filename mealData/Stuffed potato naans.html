Stuffed potato naans

Dinner, Indian, Lunch

Total Time: 50 Servings: 2 Source: Jamie 7 Ways

Ingredients

300g self raising flour

150 ml water

250g potato

2 onions

300g frozen spinach

1 tbsp madras paste

2 tsp mango chutney

2 tbsp natural yoghurt

40g feta

Directions

1) Put flour into a large bowl with a pinch of sea salt
2) Add water and mix into dough, kneading for 3 minutes, leave to rest.
3) For the filling, peel the potato and onions and chop into 3cm chunks
4) Cook in a pan of boiling water for 10 minutes then drain
5) Put spinach and curry paste (or spices) into a large non-stick pan on a low heat, cover, and leave to defrost and gently fry
6) Stir in the drained potatoes and onions, cook on a medium-high heat until golden, mashing as you go.
7) Divide your dough in two, shape into balls then stretch and flatten into 15cm rounds on a floured surface
8) Divide the cooled filling into two, shape into compact balls, sit one on each piece of dough, then gently pull the dough around the filling to seal.
9) Pat and flatten the dough , pushing it back out to 15cm
10) Cook each naan in the pan on a medium-low heat with 1 tbsp olive oil for 10 minutes or until dark golden and puffed up, turning a few times.
11) Ripple the mango chutney through the yoghurt
12) Serve the naans sprinkled with feta
