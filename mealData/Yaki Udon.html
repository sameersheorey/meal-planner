Yaki Udon

Chicken, Dinner, Japanese, Nikki

Prep Time: 10 mins Cook Time: 10 mins Total Time: 20 mins Servings: 2 Source: Drivemehungry.com

Ingredients

2 packets udon noodles

6 mushrooms sliced

5 spring onions

1 red pepper

1/2 aubergine (optional)

250g turkey mince

1 tbsp vegetable oil

Sauce

2½ tbsp dark soy sauce

2 tbsp oyster sauce

1 tbsp mirin

2 tsp brown sugar

½ tsp rice wine vinegar

Description

Yaki udon is an easy Japanese stir fried udon noodle recipe ready in 20 minutes! Stir fried udon noodles with a savoury 5 ingredient yaki udon noodle sauce.

Directions

1) Mix all the ingredients for the sauce in a small bowl and set aside.

2) Heat your pan over high heat and add the oil. Add in the turkey mince and stir fry for 1 minute

3) Add the rest of the vegetables, stir fry together for 1 minute.

4) Add in the noodles and sauce. Continue stir frying for 3 - 4 minutes or until well combined. Serve immediately.
