Chickpea tikka masala

Dinner, Indian, Vegan

Servings: 4

Ingredients

4 brown onions

800g cooked chickpeas

2 tbsp tandoori masala

4 cloves garlic

2 tsp ground paprika

80g mango chutney

100g creamed coconut

500ml water (for stock)

4 tomatoes

4 tbsp tomato puree

2 vegetable stock cubes

260g basmati rice

600 ml water (for rice)


Optional

20g fresh coriander

30g flaked almonds

Directions

1) Peel and finely chop the onion

2) Add rice and cold water to pan and bring to boil, once boiling cooked covered on low heat for 10-12 mins. When cooked remove from heat and keep covered until serving

3) Heat a large wide-based pan with a drizzle of vegetable oil over a medium heat, once hot add onions and cook for 6-8 minutes until soft

4) Drain chickpeas and chop tomatoes finely

5) Once the onion is soft, add tomatoes and crush garlic inot pan, cook for 4-5 mins

6) Boil a kettle, dissolve stock cubes, creamed coconut and tomato puree in boiling water

7) Add paprika, tandoori masala and chickpeas to pan, cook for 1-2 mins

8) Add coconut stock and mango chutney, cook for 6-7 minutes until thickened

9) Serve rice with curry and top with flaked almonds and chopped coriander (if using)
