Spicy Sweet Potato, Squash and Lentil Soup

Lunch

Prep Time: 15 minutes Cook Time: 40 minutes Servings: 4-6 Source: Somethingsweetsomethingsavoury.com

Ingredients

1 tbsp vegetable oil

1 onion

1 stick of celery

1 carrot

1 red pepper

250g sweet potatoes

250g butternut squash

2 cloves of garlic

1 ginger cube

1 tsp ground cumin

1/2 tsp chilli flakes

200g red lentils, rinsed

1 vegetable stock cube

200ml coconut milk

Directions

1) Dice the onion

2) Heat the oil over a low to medium heat and add the diced onion. Turn the heat to low and cook until the onions are translucent (about 5-10 minutes)

3) Dice the celery, carrot, red pepper, squash and sweet potato

4) Once the onion is soften add the diced vegetables

5) Sprinkle with salt and gently fry for about 10 minutes or until they are beginning to soften.

6) Dissolve stock cube in 1/1.5 l hot water

6) Add the crushed garlic, ginger, ground cumin and chilli flakes and cook for a further couple of minutes.

7) Rinse the lentils and add with the stock. Bring to the boil, then turn down the heat to medium low. Place a lid on the pan and cook for 30 minutes.

8) Pour in the coconut milk and continue to cook for a further 10 minutes.

9) Blend the soup to a consistency you're happy with. Season with salt and freshly ground black pepper.
