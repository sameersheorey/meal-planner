Loaded BBQ Chicken Nachos

Chicken, Dinner, Mexican

Prep Time: 20 Cook Time: 15 Total Time: 35 Servings: 4 Source: Tasty.co

Ingredients

1 tbsp vegetable oil

2 chicken breast, finely diced

½ tbsp salt

½ tbsp freshly ground black pepper

1 tbsp garlic powder

1 tbsp chili powder

180ml BBQ sauce

1 bag (~370g) tortilla chips

200g grated cheese

½ red onion, finely sliced

1 tomato, diced

100g sour cream

1 avocado, diced

Directions

1) Preheat oven to 176°C.

2) Heat the oil in a pan over high heat. Cook the chicken with salt, pepper, garlic powder, and chili powder for two to three minutes.

3) Add the barbecue sauce and cook until the sauce has reduced by about half, about five minutes. Remove from heat.

4) In a large ovenproof dish, spread out half the tortilla chips. Layer with half of the barbecue chicken, followed by half of the cheese, onion and tomato.

5) Repeat with the remaining ingredients.

6) Bake for 15 minutes.

7) Drizzle the sour cream and sprinkle the avocado on top. Serve!
